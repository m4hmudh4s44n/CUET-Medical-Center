Ensuring Health for All

We provide free health check-ups and essential medicines to all members of our community. Our goal is to ensure everyone has access to basic healthcare and the support they need to stay healthy.

  • Regular free health check-up camps
  • Distribution of essential medicines
  • Consultation with experienced doctors
  • Health awareness programs
  • Support for chronic disease management
